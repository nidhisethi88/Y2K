Y2K - Yogaposes2Korrect

Impacting the mental as well as physical health of individuals

What is exactly Y2K?

Y2K or Yogaposes2Korrect is a website which aims at improving the mental and physical health of people in Pandemic times.It is a yoga trainer in your own devices which helps you to perform yoga poses and estimates the accuracy.

How does it work?

The machine learning model calculates the accuracy of the yoga postures based on some calculations at the back-end.

Can anyone use it?

Yes! Anyone sitting at their homes can access the website with just a click.
